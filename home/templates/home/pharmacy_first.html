Conditions we can treat as part of Pharmacy First are

Ear Infection

If your child has pain in the ear, they may have an infection (Otitis Media). Bring them into our pharmacy to see our Pharmacist or book an appointment. This service is for children aged one year to seventeen years.

Impetigo

Impetigo is a skin infection that's very contagious but not usually serious. It often gets better in 7 to 10 days with treatment. Anyone can get it, but it is very common in young children. Use our Pharmacy First service for quick help with Impetigo.

Insect Bite

Patients aged one year and over who have an infected insect bite can receive support from the Pharmacist and team. If the bite area is red, itchy, tender or swollen, visit our pharmacy today.

Shingles

Shingles is a viral infection that manifests as a painful rash, more common in the elderly. It is caused by the same virus as chickenpox and can leave lasting nerve pain. Early treatment is key to managing this uncomfortable condition.

Sore Throat

Sore throats are frequent but usually nothing to worry about. They typically resolve on their own within a week. Our Pharmacy First service can help you recover more quickly.

UTIs

UTIs are usually caused by bacteria entering the urinary tract through the urethra. Women have a shorter urethra, making it easier for bacteria to reach the bladder or kidneys and cause infection.

Sinusitis

Sinusitis is the painful swelling of the sinus cavities, commonly triggered by infection. It typically resolves within a month, but if symptoms persist, medication can speed recovery.

About Pharmacy First

We can provide you the same treatments you would get from a GP.

The pharmacy first service is an accessible service for all, where you can receive support, advice and if necessary, medication to support you with common conditions.
