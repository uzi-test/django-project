What is COVID-19 ?

COVID-19 is a respiratory illness that is spread through coughs, sneezes and close contact with people who have the virus. Symptoms can include a high temperature or shivering, a new continuous cough, a sore throat, and a loss or change in sense of smell or taste.

Most people have mild symptoms and recover quickly, but some people can become severely ill. Occasionally, symptoms can persist and develop into a condition called “Long COVID”.

Private COVID-19 Vaccination

We offer a private COVID-19 Vaccination Service for adults and children aged 12 years and over. Please note that this is not an NHS service. The vaccination helps protect against catching COVID-19 and passing it on to others.

This service isn’t suitable for anyone who has had:

  • A COVID-19 vaccination within the last 3 months
  • An allergic reaction to any injections or vaccinations in the past

This isn’t a complete list and suitability will be checked before the vaccination is given.

A FREE NHS COVID-19 Vaccination
