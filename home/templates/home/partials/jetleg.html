Jet Lag Treatment

Jet lag is a temporary sleep disorder that occurs when your body’s internal clock becomes misaligned with the time zone you’ve travelled to. It’s most common after long-haul flights that cross multiple time zones and can cause fatigue, headaches, and difficulty concentrating.

Cockerton Pharmacy – Jet Lag treatment and advice available in Darlington, Newcastle upon Tyne, Wallsend and across the UK.

About this Service

We offer over-the-counter medication and professional advice to help alleviate the symptoms of jet lag. Supplements such as melatonin can regulate sleep patterns, while caffeine can provide a temporary energy boost. We may also suggest natural remedies like lavender oil or chamomile tea to help relax the body and promote restful sleep. With the right solutions, the pharmacy can be a traveler’s best friend in combating jet lag.
