What is a private prescription?

A private prescription is a service that allows you to obtain prescription medications directly without visiting your GP's practice.

This streamlined approach is ideal for individuals with busy schedules or limited access to healthcare facilities. Our service aims to make your healthcare journey more convenient and efficient.

Cockerton Pharmacy – Your trusted partner for health and wellbeing in Darlington, Newcastle upon Tyne, and Wallsend.

How does our private prescription service work?

Step 1.

Online Consultation

We will send you a link in your booking confirmation email to complete a patient questionnaire and provide relevant details about your medical history, current symptoms, and any medications you may be taking. This information helps our healthcare professionals gain a comprehensive understanding of your situation.

Step 2.

Professional Evaluation

Regular physical activity, such as brisk walking, jogging, swimming, or cycling, can help you lose weight. Our qualified prescribers will thoroughly review your information. They will assess your condition and contact you to discuss this. If appropriate, they will recommend suitable treatment options tailored to your needs. Our experienced team ensures that you receive expert guidance and appropriate prescriptions.

Step 3.

Prescription

If our prescribers determine that a prescription is necessary, we will issue it electronically and send it to one of our branches for you to collect. You can also have this sent to another pharmacy of your choice. You will need to pay for the medication prescribed when you collect it.

Step 4.

Ongoing Support

Our commitment to your well-being extends beyond issuing prescriptions. We offer ongoing support and are available to address any questions or concerns you may have regarding your treatment. Our team is dedicated to providing the highest level of care throughout your healthcare journey.
