Which vaccinations will I need to protect me in the country I am visiting ?

When planning a trip to a new country, one of the most important things to consider is which vaccinations you will need. Different regions of the world have different diseases and health risks, so it's crucial to protect yourself by getting the necessary shots. Some common vaccinations to ask about include those for yellow fever, hepatitis A and B, typhoid, and malaria.

It’s also important to inquire about any required vaccinations for entry into the country, such as for COVID-19. Don’t forget to schedule your vaccinations at least 4-6 weeks before your trip to ensure full protection. Your health and safety should always be a top priority while traveling.

Travel Services

Ready to embark on your next adventure? Book a travel related service today and let us take care of all your specific requirements.
Altitude Sickness Treatment

As you set off on your high-altitude adventures, don’t let altitude sickness get in the way of your enjoyment. Our pharmacy is proud to offer a comprehensive Altitude Sickness Treatment Service to help manage and prevent this condition.

Cholera

Cholera disease is a highly contagious bacterial infection that causes severe diarrhoea, vomiting, and dehydration. It can be fatal if not treated promptly with antibiotics and rehydration therapy.

DTP

Diphtheria, tetanus, and polio are all dangerous diseases that can have severe impacts on people’s health. Vaccines like DTP are crucial in protecting against these deadly illnesses.

Dengue Fever

Dengue Fever is a mosquito-borne viral infection common in tropical regions. Protect yourself with the Qdenga vaccine, available at our pharmacy. Get expert advice and stay safe before you travel.

Diarrhoea

Diarrhoea is passing looser, watery or more frequent stools than is normal for you. It affects most people from time to time and is usually nothing to worry about. It can be distressing and unpleasant. It normally clears quickly or our pharmacist can help you.

Hepatitis A

Hepatitis A is a highly contagious liver disease caused by the hepatitis A virus. It can be prevented through vaccination and proper hygiene practices such as washing hands regularly.

Hepatitis B

Hepatitis B is a serious disease caused by a virus that attacks the liver, leading to inflammation and potentially life-threatening complications if left untreated. It can be prevented by vaccination.

Japanese Encephalitis

A mosquito-borne viral infection found mainly in rural Asia. Protect yourself with the Japanese Encephalitis vaccine before traveling.

Jet Lag

Jet lag, also known as desynchronosis, is a common experience for travelers crossing multiple time zones. It can cause fatigue, insomnia, and difficulty concentrating.

MMR Vaccine

Protects against Measles, Mumps, and Rubella — three contagious viral infections that can lead to serious complications. Essential for both children and adults.

Malaria Prevention

Malaria is a mosquito-borne disease that can cause severe illness. Preventive medicines and mosquito protection are key to staying safe while traveling.

Meningitis ACWY (MenACWY)

Meningitis ACWY (MenACWY) is a serious bacterial infection that can cause meningitis and septicaemia (blood poisoning). The MenACWY vaccine protects against the A, C, W, and Y strains of meningococcal bacteria. It’s recommended for teenagers and can be given up to age 25.

Meningitis B (MenB)

Meningitis B (MenB) is a serious bacterial infection that can cause meningitis and septicaemia. It’s most common in babies and young children, but can affect people of any age.

Tick-borne Encephalitis

Tick-borne Encephalitis is a serious disease caused by a virus carried by ticks. Symptoms include fever, headache, and brain inflammation — making early detection and prevention crucial.

Typhoid

Typhoid is a highly contagious disease caused by Salmonella bacteria, spread through contaminated food and water. Symptoms include high fever, abdominal pain, and diarrhoea. Vaccination is recommended for prevention.

Yellow Fever is a viral disease transmitted by mosquitoes, causing symptoms such as fever, headache, and jaundice. It can be prevented by getting vaccinated and avoiding mosquito bites.
